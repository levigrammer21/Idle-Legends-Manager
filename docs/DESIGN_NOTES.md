## World

Central hub: Kingswatch.

Four major surrounding cities:
- North: Irondeep
- East: Greenhaven
- South: Saltshore
- West: Emberfall

Special locations:
- Elder Woods, locked behind quest
- Griffin Nest, future boss/mount chase
- Broken Quarry, rare resource site

## Economy

Direct player-to-player trading rejected. Everything goes through market systems.
Bosses drop components. Players craft equipment.
Lower-tier resources and tools are consumed in higher-tier recipes.
